About Prashnat Tamang

Prashant Tamang (1983â€“2026) was an Indian singer and actor of Nepali origin who became a cultural icon after winning Indian Idol Season 3 in 2007. His victory was a historic moment for the Gorkha community, sparking a massive wave of regional pride and unity across the Darjeeling hills, Sikkim, and the global Nepali diaspora.

Achievement

  • Indian Idol Victory: He was crowned the winner of Indian Idol Season 3 on September 23, 2007, making him the first singer of Nepali origin to win the prestigious national title.
  • Massive Public Mandate: He secured his victory in the grand finale with approximately 70 million votes, a record-breaking margin (roughly 10 times more than the runner-up).
  • Cultural Iconography: He was affectionately titled "Pahar ka Gaurav" (Pride of the Hills) for uniting the Gorkha diaspora across India, Nepal, and globally through his journey.
  • Successful Debut Album: Following his win, he released his maiden album Dhanyavad (Thank You) with Sony BMG, which featured hits in both Hindi and Nepali.
